Cheesecake Recipe

Description

An easy cheesed based desert to top off your day of cheese-eating

Ingredients

  • Block cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • Sour cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl until creamy.
  2. Place in a container and bake at 220C for 2 hours.
  3. Let cool for 30mins to 1 hour.
