Lasagna Recipe

Description

A scrumptious homemade lasagna that's bound to make mouths water

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. In an oven, cook meat, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in tomato sauce and water. Season with Italian seasoning, stirring occasionally.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
