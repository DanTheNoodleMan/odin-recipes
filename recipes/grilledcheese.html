Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

A home favorite for when you're not bothered to spend 30 minutes cooking

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Cheese of choice
  • Seasoning of choice

Steps

  1. In between two slices of bread place your sliced cheese.
  2. In a toastie maker or alternatively a frying pan, place your bread containing cheese.
  3. Fry or cook until golden.
